Indonesia to Host ASEAN Climate Summit 2025

Rina Wulandari • Politics • June 24, 2025

Jakarta, Indonesia -

Indonesia has officially been selected as the host country for the ASEAN Climate Summit 2025, marking a historic milestone in the nation's diplomatic and environmental efforts.

The announcement was made during the ASEAN Ministerial Meeting held in Kuala Lumpur last weekend. Indonesia's proposal stood out for its ambitious commitment to renewable energy.

A Regional Focus on Climate Action

The summit scheduled for November 2025 will bring together leaders and environmental ministers from all 10 ASEAN member states.
